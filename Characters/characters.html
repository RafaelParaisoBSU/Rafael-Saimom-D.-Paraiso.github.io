Kyle Crane

Kyle Crane is the protagonist of Dying Light and Dying Light: The Following, and is mentioned in Dying Light 2 Stay Human. Crane is hired by the GRE to hunt down Kadir Suleiman and retrieve a file stolen from the agency.

Jade Aldemir

Jade Aldemir is a major character in Dying Light. She is a runner for The Tower by securing airdrops in The Slums. She was once known as the popular kickboxer "The Scorpion" before the outbreak occurred.

Harris Brecken

Brecken is an ex-Parkour instructor who came to Harran. He is the Tower's leader, the Courtyard's Spike and Dr. Zere both affiliated with him. He has strong beliefs and strength which people look up to.

Rahim Aldemir

Rahim is a native of Harran and the younger brother of world kickboxing champion Jade Aldemir. He assists Harris Brecken, leader of the survivors residing inside the Tower, with managing the runners and scouts as well as planning operations.

Dr. Imran Zere

Dr. Imran Zere, a scientist working on a cure for the infection, typically stays inside his camper near the Tower. The trailer is guarded by several armed survivors and is a default safe zone.

Kadir Suleiman

Kadir Suleiman, better known as Rais, is a main character featured in Dying Light, serving as the primary antagonist. Suleiman is a former colonel in the military tasked with maintaining order within the quarantine as acting governor for the city of Harran.
