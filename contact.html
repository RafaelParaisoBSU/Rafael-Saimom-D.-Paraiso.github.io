Techland S.A. is a Polish video game developer and publisher founded in 1991 by Paweł Marchewka. It developed Call of Juarez and its prequel Call of Juarez: Bound in Blood, as well as Dead Island and Dying Light. The company is headquartered in Wrocław in western Poland and has offices in Wrocław and Warsaw
